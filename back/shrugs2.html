Shrugs





Cable Silverback Shrugs



Difficulty: Beginner





1.Use a bar attachment. Cable should be set all the way to the bottom.

2.Stand up with the attachment in hand, walk a couple of steps back make sure you are standing upright and not leaning backwards.

3.Retract your shoulder blades and then protract to return to the starting position.


Cable 30 Degree Shrug



Difficulty: Beginner





1.Use a handle attachment on both sides of crossover. With cable attachment set all the way to the bottom.

2.Grab both handles and make sure you are centered in the machine. Let your arms hang freely.

3.Pull your shoulder blades up and in towards your ears. Let your shoulder blades depress back to the starting position.
