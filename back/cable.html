Cable Pull in


Difficulty: Intermediate




1.Use a handle attachment set all the way to the top of the cable crossover machine.

2.Walk a few steps away from the cable machine. Face your body sideways.

3.Face your palm up toward the ceiling. Then pull your elbow in toward your rib cage.
