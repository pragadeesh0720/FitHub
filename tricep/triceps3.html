Dumbell Triceps Workouts


Dumbell Skullcrusher

"Dumbbell flat skullcrusher, also known as dumbbell lying triceps extension: a weightlifting exercise that targets the triceps muscles by lowering and lifting dumbbells to the sides of the head while lying on a bench."



1.Lay flat on the floor or a bench with your fists extended to the ceiling and a neutral grip.

2.Break at the elbows until your fists are by your temples. Then extend your elbows and flex your triceps at the top

Dumbell Seated Overhead Tricep Extension

"Dumbbell overhead tricep extension: a weightlifting exercise that targets the triceps muscles by lifting a dumbbell overhead with both hands and then lowering the weight behind the head while keeping the upper arms stationary."



1.Sit on the bench and hold a dumbbell with both hands. Raise the dumbbell overhead at arms length, holding the weight up with the palms of your hands.

2.Keep your elbows in while you lower the weight behind your head, your upper arms stationary.

3.Raise the weight back to starting position.

Dumbell Decline Skullcrusher

"Dumbbell decline skullcrusher, also known as decline dumbbell triceps extension: a weightlifting exercise that targets the triceps muscles by lowering and lifting dumbbells behind the head while lying on a decline bench."



1.Start by lying on a decline bench with a dumbbell in each hand.

Hold the dumbbells above your chest with your arms extended and your palms facing each other. This is the starting position.
