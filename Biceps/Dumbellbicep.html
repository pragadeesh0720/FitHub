Biceps Dumbell Workouts



Dumbell curl


Dumbbell curls are some of the most basic strength-training exercises you can learn.





1.Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand at arm's length.

2.Raise one dumbbell and twist your forearm until it is vertical and your palm faces the shoulder.

3.Lower to original position and repeat with opposite arm.

Dumbell Hammer Curl


Bicep hammer curls are a type of weightlifting exercise that targets the biceps muscles in the upper arm.
They are performed by holding a dumbbell with a neutral grip (palms facing in) and curling the weight towards the shoulder, keeping the elbows close to the body.





1.Hold the dumbbells with a neutral grip (thumbs facing the ceiling).

2.Slowly lift the dumbbell up to chest height

3.Return to starting position and repeat.

Dumbell Incline Hammer Curl


Bicep dumbbell hammer curls involve holding a dumbbell in each hand with a neutral grip and curling the weights towards the shoulders,
targeting the biceps muscles while keeping the elbows close to the body.
